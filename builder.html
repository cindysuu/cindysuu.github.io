Futures

React Native | Clerk | Convex | Modal

A financial literacy app for kids, made for HackMIT.

Inspiration

I recently came to the uncomfortable realization that I know nothing about personal finances‚Äìhow to manage my spending, how to choose a credit card, and most importantly, how to invest. This led me and a few friends to the idea of building a financial literacy platform for children to learn investing fundamentals through an engaging experience, using virtual gems for trading real-time stocks from companies like Netflix, McDonald‚Äôs, Disney, and more. Kids can then cash in their returns through a gem-to-dollar exchange rate that a parent account can set, not only providing great incentive to wait and make a return on their investment but also minimizing parents' risks of losing any substantial amount of money. I'm proud that we were able to build this idea within 24 hours for HackMIT and especially enjoyed the challenge of making this a kid-friendly adaptation (cross-platform, fun animations, easy-to-understand stock trends, LLM-generated financial trends).
