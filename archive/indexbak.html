Programming

LOC8

Find My, but better. LOC8 makes it easier than ever to see what your friends are up to on campus, helping social interactions among college students be more casual and spontaneous. Made using React Native and JavaScript with Firebase for account management. Created at HackPrinceton because my friends and I thought that Find My had limited features. Learn more here.

Juve

Application that helps elderly individuals recover from physical therapy by harnessing the universal appeal of games with movement. Made using Python, HTML/CSS/JS and OpenCV at HackHarvard. Learn more here.

Bridge4Good

A website dedicated to connecting homeless shelters with the resources they need during COVID-19. Developed in a team of five at the Teens in AI #YouthVSCovid Hackathon and won 3rd place. Made with a web app hosting platform with Flask to deploy back-end functionality. Learn more here and visit the website here.
